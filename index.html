The Story

Happiest
Emo Fairy

"We have to feel to heal."

Meghan Kylie is a singer-songwriter, music educator, poet, energy work practitioner, and performer based in Philadelphia, PA. She creates music and healing that are an homage to the human experience.

Music has been her lifeline since age 5. Trauma and PTSD tried to silence her — but songwriting became a daily ritual of survival and joy. In 2024, she left a 9-5 to go full-time creative, releasing debut album THIEF: a love letter to imposter syndrome, self-worth, and the queer heart.

Whether belting originals in Philly venues, teaching voice/piano, or weaving Reiki into performances — her mission is simple: come feel something real.
